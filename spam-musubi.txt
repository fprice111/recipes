1 can of spam, sliced into 8 pcs.
16 oz of white rice
8 sheets of sushi/musubi nori
furikake

cook rice.  cook spam on med hi heat until a nice golden brown color is attained.  form rice or use a musubi press.  lightly sprinkle furikake on musubi.  place spam on musubi.  wrap musubi in nori.  enjoy.
